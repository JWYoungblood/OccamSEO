The Occam Philosophy

The simplest
explanation
is usually right.

Most SEO agencies thrive on complexity. More deliverables, more reports, more meetings — all signals of value, but not value itself.

We believe the opposite. Occam's Razor says: when two solutions explain the same problem, the simpler one wins. We apply that principle relentlessly to SEO strategy.

Fewer, higher-quality pages beat a thousand thin ones. A handful of powerful links outperform hundreds of weak ones. A clear site architecture beats complex taxonomy every time.
