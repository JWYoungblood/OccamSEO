FAQ

Common questions about
SEO Audits.

How is your audit different from a free tool like Semrush or Ahrefs?

Automated tools are good at finding issues — they're not good at telling you which ones actually matter. Our value is in the prioritization and interpretation: knowing which of those 300 flagged issues will genuinely move your rankings, and which are noise. We also add competitive context that automated tools can't provide.

How long does an SEO audit take?

For most small to mid-sized sites, we deliver within 10–14 business days. Larger sites with thousands of pages or complex technical setups may take longer. We'll give you a precise timeline after an initial scoping call.

Do you help implement the fixes after the audit?

Yes. Many clients engage us to implement the audit findings as a follow-on project. We can work alongside your existing team or handle implementation directly depending on your setup and platform.

What do I need to provide for the audit?

Access to Google Search Console and Google Analytics is essential. Everything else — crawl data, backlink data, competitor analysis — we pull ourselves. The more access we have to your analytics history, the more precise our findings will be.
