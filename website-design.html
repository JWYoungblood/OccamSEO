Our Process

From strategy
to live site.

01

SEO Strategy & Architecture Planning

Before any design work begins, we map the site's information architecture: what pages exist, what keywords they target, how they link together, and what schema markup each will carry.

02

Design & Wireframing

We design the site with both user experience and conversion in mind — clean layouts, clear hierarchy, and CTAs placed where they perform. You review and approve before we write a line of code.

03

Development & SEO Implementation

We build clean, hand-coded HTML/CSS with all SEO elements implemented correctly from the start — schema, canonical tags, meta, performance optimization, and structured internal linking.

04

Launch & Search Console Setup

We handle launch, submit to Google Search Console, verify indexation, and monitor the site's initial crawl to ensure everything is indexed correctly from day one.

FAQ

Common questions about
Website Design.

What platform do you build on?

We build hand-coded HTML/CSS/JS sites for clients who want maximum performance and simplicity, and WordPress sites for clients who need a CMS to manage their own content. All our WordPress builds are performance-optimized and stripped of unnecessary plugins. We recommend the platform that fits your needs, not the one that's easiest for us.

How long does a website project take?

For a typical small business site (5–10 pages), we deliver in 4–6 weeks from strategy kickoff to live launch. Larger sites with more complex functionality or content requirements take longer. We'll give you a precise timeline in your proposal.

Will I be able to edit the site myself?

Yes, if that's what you need. WordPress builds are designed for client editing with a simple, clean admin interface. For static HTML sites, we can provide instructions or be available for updates on a retainer basis.

Do you offer ongoing SEO after the site launches?

Yes. Our other services — Technical SEO, Content Strategy, Link Building, and Local SEO — all pair naturally with a new website. Many clients engage us to continue building rankings after launch. We can discuss an ongoing engagement as part of your project proposal.
