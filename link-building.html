Our Principles

What we will never do —
and why it matters.

01 — Never

Private Blog Networks

PBN links are a short-term tactic with long-term consequences. Google identifies and devalues PBN links continuously — and penalizes the sites using them. We don't build them, ever.

02 — Never

Paid Link Schemes

Paying for links directly violates Google's guidelines and creates a liability that can be triggered at any algorithm update. We earn links, we don't buy them.

03 — Never

Irrelevant Links

A link from an unrelated site in an unrelated niche contributes almost nothing to your topical authority. We target only sources with genuine topical relevance to your site.

FAQ

Common questions about
Link Building.

How many links will you build per month?

We don't commit to a fixed monthly link count, because link quality matters far more than volume. For most clients, we target 3–8 high-quality placements per month. A single DR 70 link from a relevant authority site is worth more than 50 directory links.

How long does it take to see results from link building?

Links take time to be crawled, indexed, and have their full authority passed. Most clients see measurable ranking movement 2–3 months after links are acquired, with compounding effect over 6–12 months as authority builds.

What types of sites do you target for links?

Industry publications, relevant blogs with real audiences, local business directories (for local SEO), resource pages, and news sites where relevant. All targets are evaluated for domain authority, organic traffic, and topical relevance before we pursue them.

Do you do guest posting?

Yes, where the publication is a genuine, editorially-reviewed source with real traffic. We do not place guest posts on sites that exist purely to sell links — these are devalued by Google and carry penalty risk.
